Having a document read to you

You can have CocoaPad read your text to you. No, you can't speak the text and have it appear in your document. You'll need some $400 program (like Dragon NaturallySpeaking, which ain't for Macs, by the way) that do this.

To have CocoaPad read your document:

  • Choose Start Speaking from the Edit > Speech menu.

If you select some text, CocoaPad will only read the selected text. You can also have CocoaPad start over in the middle of reading by selecting Start Speaking again.
