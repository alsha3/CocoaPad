Adjusting the text alignment

If you need to centralize your text, align it to the right, or try your hand at page layout, you can adjust the text alignment in two ways:

  1. Pull down the Text menu.
  2. Select either Align Left, Center, Justify, or Align Right.

Or:

  • Click one of the 4 alignment buttons (Align Left, Center, Justify, or Align Right) on the ruler.

If you don't select any text to be aligned, all text in between the first and last Return in the paragraph/line is aligned.
