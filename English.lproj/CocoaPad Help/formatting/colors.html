Playing with colors

That same dull black—wouldn't it be nice to make your text a different color? Like orange or something?

To change the text color:

  1. Click the Colors button on the toolbar, or pull down the Font menu, and select Show Colors (or press Command-Shift-C).
  2. The color picker will appear. Choose the color you want your text to be.
  3. Click the close button to close the color picker.

Tip: Colors can be applied immediately to selected text.
