Changing the text style

You'd probably want to put spice up your text even more, by having text italic, bold, underlined, etc. It's easy to do so.

To change the text style:

  • Pull down the Font menu, and select either Bold (Command-B), Italic (Command-I), or Underline (Command-U).

Or:

  • On the toolbar, click either Bold, Italic, or Underline.

Tip: Styles can be applied immediately to selected text.
