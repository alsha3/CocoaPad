About CocoaPad

CocoaPad is a simple Mac OS X word processor that's one-of-a-kind. It can do regular word processing, supports images, can open RTF and text files, and it also has a few features of its own (like CAPITALIZING text).

About this version

Version 1.1 fixes a couple of annoying bugs from the initial release:

  • CocoaPad now opens Word documents correctly.
  • The bug where saving makes the cursor revert to the place you last typed has been fixed.

The Joy of Open Source

For other programmers who want to see a real program's code, so they can get started on their own programs, CocoaPad is Open Source. That means anyone can see the source. That also means they can discuss bugs, improve it, etc. Of course, they could steal it, but this is such a simple program, it wouldn't be worth much.
