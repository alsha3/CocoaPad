If it really is a bug…

You should report what happened immediately to me, so I can try to fix it as soon as possible.

  1. Fire up your e-mail client (or go to your web-based e-mail site).
  2. Send an e-mail to betacheese@gmail.com describing what the problem was, when the problem occurred, and what the situation was. Be sure to mention that this is a problem related to CocoaPad (preferably in the Topic field). See the sample bug report below.

Here's a sample bug report:

CocoaPad crashes when I change the Check Spelling As You Type preference and then click Apply To All. I usually have only 1 or 2 documents open at a time. I open the Preferences, click the Check Spelling As You Type checkbox, click Apply To All, then click Apply To All again to confirm, and CocoaPad crashes! I tried restarting CocoaPad, throwing away the preferences file, logging out, and reinstalling CocoaPad. Any ideas?
